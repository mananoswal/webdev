Our Story

Nestled in the heart of South Delhi, Coffee Cafe 9 began in 2015 as a cosy neighbourhood café where passion for fine brews meets everyday stories. What started in Malviya Nagar with a tiny espresso machine has grown into a creative hub for artists, freelancers, and coffee enthusiasts across Delhi NCR.

We roast premium beans from Coorg, Araku Valley and Chikmagalur, serve handcrafted beverages, and pair them with comforting Indian and continental bakes. Whether you’re brainstorming ideas, meeting friends, or seeking quiet moments — Coffee Cafe 9 is your perfect Delhi corner for warmth and inspiration.
